ROADMAP

The HEIZO roadmap outlines the long-term evolution of the ecosystem, from its token foundation to artificial intelligence, gaming experiences, blockchain infrastructure and future technologies.

The roadmap is a long-term direction, designed to evolve as HEIZO grows.

HEIZO Token

The foundation of the ecosystem and the starting point for future development, utility and community growth.

Artificial Intelligence

Development of specialized AI services designed to provide practical value and support future digital products.

Gaming Experiences

Creation of interactive games and digital experiences connected to the HEIZO ecosystem.

Blockchain Infrastructure

Long-term development of blockchain solutions intended to strengthen the ecosystem and support future expansion.

Future Technologies

Exploration of emerging technologies and new opportunities that may become part of the HEIZO ecosystem over time.
